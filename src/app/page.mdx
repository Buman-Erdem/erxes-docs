# Introduction

## What is erxes?

Welcome to the erxes developer documentation, the open-source alternative to HubSpot and Qualtrics, offering a myriad of features!

erxes is an open-source Experience Operating System (XOS) designed to empower SaaS providers and digital marketing agencies/developers to craft unique experiences across various business domains.

<Note>
  Eager to get started with erxes? Dive into our [Installation guide](/quickstart/deployment/deploymentDocker) or explore our [live demo](#)!
</Note>

## With erxes, you can...

Unleash the potential of erxes with these capabilities:

- **Open-Source Flexibility**: Utilize erxes as an open-source project, enabling individuals and companies, especially SaaS providers, to enhance various experiences such as customer service, employee management, production, and more.
- **Extend Functionality**: As digital marketing technology agencies, add value to your products and services by offering erxes privately to clients.
- **Explore the Marketplace**: Discover and distribute plugins created on erxes XOS via the [erxes Marketplace](https://erxes.io/marketplace).
